A Poetic Tribute
Oh My girl, Oh my dear lady
What I did and what Will i do
What went good or what wish got fulfilled
Which mosque I went and what did i pray
Oh My girl, oh my dear lady
What distance did to me or what distance made me feel
What is blushing to me and who made me to blush more
Why my camera captured me more and what my camera want to capture more
Oh My girl, Oh my dear lady
What made me think like a hero and who made me like a hero
What i scared of more and why I dont want to leave
What made me to think more and what made me love more
Oh My girl, Oh my dear lady
So many what and So many why
few more where, less more who
All I think about is you and All answers are pointing it to you
Oh My girl, Oh my dear lady
I wish you full of love and full of happiness
Oh My girl, Oh my dear lady
I love you always with heart full of you.
