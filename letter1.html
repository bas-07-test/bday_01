A Poetic Tribute
In the hush of the evening,
When the stars begin to shine,
I pen these words to you,
In a rhythm, soft and fine.
The moonlight casts its silver,
On the tranquil, quiet sea,
Just as your words linger,
And bring serenity to me.
Each line a gentle whisper,
Each verse a tender touch,
In this poetic letter,
I hope it means as much.
